Depression

Depression is more than just feeling “down.” It is a serious illness caused by changes in brain chemistry. Research tells us that other factors contribute to the onset of depression, including genetics, changes in hormone levels, certain medical conditions, stress, grief or difficult life circumstances.

Top 3 resources

  1. MoodGYM - Get started at home with this great do-it-yourself CBT resource that can walk you through this treatment option from your own computer.
  2. Depression – Signs and Symptoms, and stories and experiences from other Youth and Young Adults from mindyourmind.ca
  3. CCI (Centre for Clinical Interventions): Coping with depression online module and workbook

Suggestions

  1. Get help! Speak to whoever, speak to our bot, or speak to a specialist.
  2. Search online. The internet has a great number of possibilites, never give up!
  3. Don't be afraid! You're not in this alone, and you don't have to be.
