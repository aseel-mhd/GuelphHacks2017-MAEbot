About us

MAEBOT (Mental Awareness Evaluation bot) is a messaging platform that harneses the power of visual communication to avtivate teens to better their wellness. Through delivering bite sized education, reminders, and tracking messages in an engaged and personalised matter, the solution grabs the attention of our users and leads them down a path of improvement. The platform has been designed to support all teens whether they are healthy or live with a medical condition - by delivering personalised content and functionality at the righ time based on their story.

We are tapping into the power of machine learning and artificial intelligence to deliver a bot experience that evolves and improves our user's health experiences over time.

We drive user engagement in two ways:

  1. Personalisation of experience based on the users age, sex, conditions, medications, and location.
  2. Harnessing the power of messenger bot to turn health care into a continous conversation that is authentic and meaningful.
